## 📖 The Story

*Adrien's Farm* follows a young child named Adrien who discovers that their grandmother's overgrown backyard is secretly a living, breathing world — one full of seeds that remember, soil that sings, and creatures that keep score.

As Adrien tends the garden through the seasons, they learn the difference between taking and tending — and what it means to be part of something larger than yourself.

---

## 🎨 Visual Style

- **Medium**: AI-generated illustration (Midjourney + Flux) with hand-edited compositing
- **Aesthetic**: Warm gouache-like textures · botanical detail · soft impasto light
- **Palette**: Earthy greens, golden hour warmth, deep soil browns, flora pinks
- **Format**: Full-bleed spreads, designed for 8.5 × 11" print and digital reader
