# 🌻 Adrien's Farm

> *An AI-illustrated children's storybook about regenerative growing, wonder, and the living world.*

Adrien's Farm is a children's storybook project exploring regenerative agriculture, ecological wonder, and the magic of growing things. The story follows Adrien through a living farm world illustrated with AI-generated imagery in a warm, whimsical style.

## Vision

This project sits at the intersection of creative storytelling, regenerative systems education, and AI art generation. It aims to introduce children to concepts like composting, mycelium networks, seed saving, and reciprocal relationships with the land through narrative and visual wonder.

## Project Status

Early-stage — story structure and illustration pipeline under development.
